Steps

  2. In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients.


  3. In a separate medium-sized bowl, cream together the butter and sugar.


  4. Add the eggs and vanilla to the creamed butter and sugar.


  5. Beat the ingredients together well with the mixer.


  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients while continuing to mix.


  7. Mix until a dough is formed.


  8. Fold in the chocolate chips.


  9. Create 1" dough balls and place them on the baking sheet.


  10. Bake in the oven for 7-10 minutes.


  11. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 1-2 minutes.
